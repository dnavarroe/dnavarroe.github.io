Interactive dashboard with Power Bi desktop

Project description

The primary objective of this project is to develop an interactive dashboard using Power BI that allows the company to analyze and understand the utility and margin of its various products in different regions. The dashboard will provide a holistic and detailed view of key sales-related metrics, facilitating strategic decision-making.

Business Context

It is essential that the board helps solve what is requested by interested parties:

  • Lack of Detailed Visibility: Lack of detailed insight into the profitability of each product and region can limit the company's ability to make informed decisions.

  • Difficulty in Identifying Growth Opportunities: The lack of an analytical tool may hinder the identification of opportunities to maximize profit margins and increase sales in specific areas.

  • Lack of Strategic Focus: Without a clear understanding of the profitability and contribution of each product and region, the company may lack a strategic focus in making business decisions.

About the Dataset

The data set includes sales details for a company that offers many types of products in different regions.

Data Visualization

To effectively address the identified business challenges, a comprehensive three-page report has been developed. This report provides a detailed insight into critical aspects for decision-making. The structure of the report is as follows:

Page 1: Company Revenue Report

On the first sheet, there is a summary of the company's income, highlighting the top 5 incomes by customer, product, and product type. Important data such as income per period and total income are emphasized. Additionally, filters for region and product type are included to observe the income trends while taking these two variables into account.

Page 2: Profitability by Region Report

On this second sheet, a summary of the company's profitability and margin by region and period is presented. It displays the total profitability and total margin data in a table, showing the profitability and margin of each region. Additionally, two bar charts depict the margin and profitability by region and period.

Page 3: Profitability by Product Report

On this third sheet, a summary of the company's profitability and margin by product type and period is presented. It displays the total profitability and total margin data in a table, showing the profitability and margin of each product type. Additionally, two bar charts depict the margin and profitability by product type and period.

This report establishes a solid foundation for addressing the identified business problems, presenting key information in a visual and accessible manner. The implementation of this approach will enable more informed and strategic decision-making, supported by detailed and relevant data.
